How It Works

Discussion sessions every 2 weeks. You are not required to attend each time, but you are required to RSVP yes or no 24 hours prior.

  • Before: Complete the assigned reading/listening (<15 min) and RSVP on this page
  • First 5 minutes: Review our Code of Conduct together — it's a living document we co-create
  • Discussion: Actively participate and leave space for quieter voices. If you're taking a lot of airspace, step back. If you're only observing, find a way in.
  • Assume good intentions. This is a learning space and a space for us to make mistakes together.
  • Homework: At session's end, assign yourself one action item matching the theme (email a rep, volunteer with a local group, do more research, etc.)
  • Accountability: Share your homework in the Google Doc. We start each session by checking in on last session's action items.
